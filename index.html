About

I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at UMN working with Volkan Isler in the Robotic Sensor Networks Lab. Before coming to Minnesota, I graduated with a bachelors degree from Sabanci University in Istanbul. Between Jan-Aug 2019 I interned at Samsung AI Center NY. My main research interests are robotics and computer vision.
